# Data Processing Agreement for Roboto SAI

**Effective Date:** February 11, 2026

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") is entered into between Roboto SAI Inc. ("Processor") and the Customer ("Controller") pursuant to applicable data protection laws.

## 1. Definitions

- **Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
- **Processing:** Any operation performed on Personal Data
- **Controller:** The entity determining purposes and means of Processing
- **Processor:** Roboto SAI, processing data on behalf of Controller

## 2. Processing Details

### Categories of Data Subjects
- End users of Roboto SAI platform
- Customers and their employees
- Visitors to web properties

### Categories of Personal Data
- Names, email addresses, IP addresses
- Chat messages and conversation data
- Usage logs and analytics
- Device and browser information

### Processing Purposes
- Provide AI chat and code assistance services
- Generate conversation summaries
- Monitor and improve service performance
- Comply with legal obligations

### Processing Activities
- Collection, storage, analysis, and deletion
- AI model training (anonymized only)
- Security monitoring and incident response
- Backup and disaster recovery

## 3. Security Measures

Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

### Technical Measures
- Data encryption at rest (AES-256)
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits

### Organizational Measures
- Employee background checks
- Data minimization policies
- Incident response procedures
- Regular security training

## 4. Subprocessors

Processor may engage subprocessors. Current subprocessors include:

| Subprocessor | Purpose | Location |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| Supabase | Database hosting | EU/US |
| xAI | AI model inference | US |
| Cloudflare | CDN and security | Global |

Controller will be notified of new subprocessors.

## 5. Data Subject Rights

Processor shall assist Controller in fulfilling data subject rights:

- Right to access, rectify, and erase data
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

## 6. Data Breach Notification

Processor shall notify Controller without undue delay upon becoming aware of a Personal Data breach.

Notification shall include:
- Description of the breach
- Categories and number of data subjects
- Potential consequences
- Mitigation measures taken

## 7. Data Protection Impact Assessment

Processor shall assist Controller with DPIAs when required by applicable law.

## 8. Return or Deletion of Data

Upon termination of services, Processor shall:
- Return all Personal Data to Controller
- Delete all copies unless retention required by law
- Certify deletion within 30 days

## 9. Audit Rights

Controller may audit Processor's compliance annually, with 30 days notice.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to countries outside the EU/EEA with adequate protection:
- EU-US Privacy Shield Framework
- Standard Contractual Clauses
- Binding Corporate Rules

## 11. Governing Law

This DPA shall be governed by the laws of [Jurisdiction], excluding conflict of laws provisions.

## 12. Liability

Each party shall be liable for damages caused by its breach of this DPA.
